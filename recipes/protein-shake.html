Protein Shake Recipe

Here is the recipe for a protein shake!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 scoop peanut butter powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop collagen
  • 1 scoop creatine
  • 2 cups almond or coconut milk

Steps

  1. Pour milk in blender cup
  2. Add banana to blender cup
  3. Add all powders into blender cup
  4. Blend and enjoy!
